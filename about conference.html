About Conference

Kanpur Institute of Technology fosters discussions about scientific theories and findings. The multidisciplinary theme of conference (NC4IB) 2024 includes topics such as artificial intelligence (AI), cloud computing, machine learning, robotics, robotic control of machinery, automatic vehicle control, robotics, 5G communication systems, bioinformatics, manufacturing of artificial organs, development of instruments for illness diagnosis, and to explore the world of biological sciences.etc. By adapting these technologies, problems related to product development can be easily solved. Scientists, technologists, and young researchers from a variety of disciplines will come together for the National Conference on Computing, Communication Control, Informatics, and Biosciences (NC4IB) to talk about the advantages of using these cutting-edge technologies as well as their deployment, risk, and scope.
